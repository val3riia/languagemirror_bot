Welcome to Language Mirror

Language Mirror is a Telegram bot that helps you learn English through natural conversation. Our approach focuses on your interests and emotions rather than formal language instruction.

Key Features:
  • Natural conversations on topics that interest you
  • Vocabulary building through dialogue, not word lists
  • Grammar teaching as functional elements rather than technical rules
  • Support for mixed-language inputs when you don't know a word
